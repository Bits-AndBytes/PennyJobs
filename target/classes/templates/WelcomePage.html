ABOUT BITS AND BYTES

Key features of our company

Our mission

Our company helps students and young adults with finding quick jobs they can do to earn some money by our brand-new app that sorts by location so that there is a unified way to look for and post small jobs.

Our idea

We came up with this idea as there was no way completely focused on finding single small jobs for students, and this way will help people earn quick cash.

Service

Our service will provide a quick and easy way to respond to and post a small job, this way students can have more money in their pockets to afford the things they need, as not everyone can find or have the time for a part time job.
